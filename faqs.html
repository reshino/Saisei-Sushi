Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are your business hours?

We’re open daily from 01:00 PM to 2:00 AM, except for Sundays.

2. Where is Saisei located?

We are located in George Town, Penang, Malaysia. Visit our Location page for directions.

3. What type of dishes do you serve?

We specialize in premium sushi, sashimi, maki rolls, and seasonal Japanese delicacies made with the finest ingredients sourced daily.

4. Do you offer reservations?

Yes, we highly recommend making a reservation to secure your table, especially for weekend evenings or group dining.

5. Do you provide delivery or takeaway?

We offer takeaway and limited local delivery. Please check our Order page for menu options and delivery coverage.
